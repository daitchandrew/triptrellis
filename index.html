Frequently asked

Questions that usually come up before the first trip.

A few practical answers on detail level, editing, hotel awareness, and how the generator behaves once you hit build.

How detailed is the itinerary?

Each day includes specific restaurant recommendations, neighborhood routing based on your hotel, time-of-day suggestions (breakfast, lunch, dinner), and cultural insights. Think of it as a co-created itinerary that balances must-sees with authentic wandering.

Can I edit the trip after generation?

Yes, completely. Swap out activities, reorder days, add your own discoveries, remove items that don't appeal to you, and drag to change the sequence. The itinerary is a starting point, not a lockdown.

Which cities are available?

We currently support 20+ destinations across Asia, Europe, Americas, and Middle East/Africa, including Seoul, Tokyo, Paris, Barcelona, New York, Dubai, and many more. New cities are added regularly.

How does "hotel-aware" planning work?

When you provide your hotel location, we cluster activities by neighborhood to minimize transit time. Days are routed so you're not zigzagging across the city. This makes the itinerary not just beautiful, but actually playable.

Can I save my itineraries?

Yes! Your itineraries are saved locally in your browser. You can revisit, edit, and refine them anytime. No account required.

How long does it take to generate?

Typically 3-8 seconds depending on city size and your preferences. The app is thinking about hotel areas, transit connections, and timing to build a route that actually makes sense.
